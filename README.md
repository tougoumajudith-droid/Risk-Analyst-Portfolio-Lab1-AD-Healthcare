#Lab1-AD-Healthcare

In the first lab , I have simulated a specific context : 

Industry : HealthCare 
Clinic name  : CLINIC MONTREAL 

Dr. Fontaine recently received a warning letter from the Commission d'accès à l'information (CAI) following a routine inspection, indicating that the clinic may not be meeting its obligations under Quebec's Loi 25. Additionally, the clinic onboarded 19 new staff members last month and a new IT administrator set up an Active Directory environment (mtlclinic.local) to manage access. No formal security review has ever been conducted on this environment.
Client request:
"We need someone to look at our system, tell us what the risks are, make sure we are compliant with Quebec law, and give us a clear action plan. We also need a document we can show the CAI inspector if they come back."

My work is to assess risks , produce reports and support decision-making to reduce financial lost. 

Frameworks applied : NIST SP 800-30 , ISO 27001, Quebec Law 25
